In the isobar formalism the ampliture that describes the decay through a resonance $r$ is modeled as
\begin{equation}
    \A_r = F_P\,F_r\, T_r\, W_r.

\begin{equation}
    T_r = \frac{1}{m_r^2 - m_{ab}^2 - \iu m_r \Gamma_{ab}}
